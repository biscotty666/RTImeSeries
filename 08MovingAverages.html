Moving Average Smoothing

  • Moving Average of order \(m\)

\[\hat{T}_t=\frac{1}{m} \sum_{j=-k}^k y_{t+j}\]

  • Weighted Moving Averages

\[\hat{T}_t=\frac{1}{m} \sum_{j=-k}^k a_jy_{t+j}\]
